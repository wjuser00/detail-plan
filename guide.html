Presidential Palace & City Walls

July 8 · 历史主题日
总统府与古城墙
09:00

🚇 Depart for Presidential Palace

Line 10 → 安德门 → Line 1 → 新街口 → Line 2 → 大行宫站 Exit 5. ~50 min.

10号线浦口万汇城站出发，换乘至大行宫站5号口出，约50分钟

Metro 地铁

🏛️ Presidential Palace 总统府

600+ years of history: Ming prince's mansion → Qing governor's office → Taiping Heavenly Kingdom → Republic of China. Allow 2–3 hours.

600多年历史：明代汉王府 → 清代两江总督署 → 太平天国天朝宫殿 → 民国总统府。建议游览2-3小时

History 历史Book Ahead 提前预约
12:30

🍜 Lunch at 1912 Block 午餐·1912街区

Restaurants next to the Presidential Palace. Or walk to 碑亭巷 / 科巷 for local snacks.

总统府旁1912街区有各类餐厅，或步行至碑亭巷/科巷品尝本地小吃

Food 美食
14:00

🧱 Zhonghua Gate 中华门城堡

Line 2: 大行宫 → 中华门. China's largest city gate with three nested "urn courts" (瓮城). Walk on the wall.

2号线大行宫站至中华门站。中国现存最大城门，三道瓮城结构精妙

Restored historical neighbourhood. Traditional architecture, teahouses, craft shops, cafés.

紧邻中华门的修复历史街区，青砖小巷、文创小店和咖啡馆

Night View 夜景Street Food 小吃

City Wall Walk & Museum

July 9 · 城墙漫步与博物馆
漫步明城墙·南京博物院
08:30

🚇 Head to Jiming Temple 前往鸡鸣寺

⛪ Jiming Temple 鸡鸣寺

One of Nanjing's oldest temples (est. ~300 AD). Rooftop pagoda with panoramic views. ~45 min.

南京最古老的寺庙之一（始建于约300年），登顶可俯瞰全城

History 历史Views 观景
10:00

🧱 City Wall: Taicheng Section 台城段城墙漫步

Walk from 鸡鸣寺 to 玄武门. ~1.5 km on 600-year-old wall with Xuanwu Lake panorama.

从鸡鸣寺附近登城向西至玄武门，约1.5公里，俯瞰玄武湖全景

12:00

🍜 Lunch near Xuanwu Lake 玄武湖附近午餐

13:30

China's Top 3 museums. Free, reserve via WeChat. Republic-era street highlight. ~2-3 hours.

中国三大博物馆之一，免费（需微信预约）。民国街实景复原是亮点

Practical Tips

🎫 Booking 预约

  • Presidential Palace 总统府: WeChat "南京总统府", 1–7 days ahead
  • Nanjing Museum 南京博物院: Free, reserve via WeChat
  • City Wall 城墙: On-site or Meituan app

🚇 Transport 交通

  • Alipay / WeChat Pay — essential 付款必备
  • Line 10 浦口万汇城站 → downtown ~50 min
  • Didi (Chinese Uber) for convenience
  • Metro card or Alipay QR 扫码乘车

☀️ July Heat 七月防暑

  • 35°C+ and very humid 高温潮湿
  • Carry water, sunscreen, hat 防晒三件套
  • Outdoor activities: morning or evening
  • Museums & malls = midday refuges 避暑

📱 Useful Apps 实用App

  • Alipay / WeChat — payments 支付
  • 高德地图 — best navigation 导航
  • 大众点评 — restaurant reviews 美食
  • 小红书 — travel tips 攻略
  • Didi — ride-hailing 打车
