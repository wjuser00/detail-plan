Flavours
What We'll Eat
No offal, no heavy spice. Nanjing food is subtle, savoury, and centuries old.
🦆

Saltwater Duck

盐水鸭

Nanjing's #1 dish. Tender, silky, lightly salted. 2,000+ years of history.

✅ Not spicy · No offal
🥟

Soup Dumplings

小笼包 / 汤包

Paper-thin skin, burst of hot savoury soup. Dip in vinegar + ginger.

✅ Great for breakfast
🍜

Pidu Noodles

皮肚面

Nanjing's own — fried pork skin in rich broth. Only found here.

✅ Very filling
🫘

Red Bean Dessert

赤豆元宵

Warm sweet red bean soup with glutinous rice balls. Comfort in a bowl.

🍬 Dessert
🫓

Jianbing

煎饼

Savoury Chinese crepe — egg, scallions, crispy cracker. Street food perfection.

🌅 Breakfast staple
☕

Café Culture

咖啡馆

Great specialty coffee scene. Perfect for midday heat escapes.

❄️ Air-conditioned

🏯 City Wall

Zhonghua Gate — world's largest surviving city gate. Ming Dynasty, 600+ years. Full wall: 35km.

Ming Dynasty · 600 years · world's longest

🏛️ Nanjing Museum

Top 3 in China. 430,000 artifacts. Dinosaurs to Republic-era. Free entry.

top 3 museums · 430,000 · free

🏮 Qinhuai River

"Mother river." Du Mu's poem. 1,000+ years of cultural history.

mother river · Du Mu · Tang Dynasty
