Preparation
Talking About Nanjing
Key English phrases and facts for each place — so you can explain things with confidence.

🏯 City Wall

Zhonghua Gate is the largest surviving city gate in the world. Ming Dynasty, 600+ years old. The full wall is 35km — longest ancient city wall on Earth.

Keywords: Ming Dynasty · 600 years · world's longest · 35km

🏛️ Nanjing Museum

One of China's top 3 museums. 430,000 artifacts. From dinosaurs to Republic-era street scenes. Free entry.

Keywords: top 3 museums · 430,000 · free entry

🏮 Qinhuai River

Nanjing's "mother river." Tang poet Du Mu wrote about its misty, moonlit beauty. 1,000+ years of cultural history.

Keywords: mother river · Du Mu · Tang Dynasty

☕ Keemun & Britain

Keemun black tea from Qimen, Anhui. Reached Britain in the 19th century, became a royal favourite.

Keywords: Qimen · Anhui · 19th century · royal favourite

🦆 Nanjing & Ducks

Nanjing people eat over 100 million ducks a year. Saltwater duck has 2,000+ years of history.

Keywords: 100 million ducks · 2,000 years · duck museum
