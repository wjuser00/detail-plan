Explore Nanjing探索日探索日 Explore Nanjing

Thursday, July 9 · History & culture历史与文化
~08:00

🥟 Chinese breakfast experience🥟 中国早餐体验

Soup dumplings, youtiao (fried dough sticks), jianbing (savory crepe), soy milk — no bread, no cereal. This is how China starts the day. 汤包、油条、煎饼、豆浆——没有面包和麦片。这就是中国的一天之始。

~09:30

🏯 Nanjing City Wall — Zhonghua Gate🏯 南京城墙·中华门

The largest surviving ancient city gate in the world. Built 600+ years ago in the Ming Dynasty. Walk atop the wall and feel the layers of history. 35km of wall — we'll explore a section at a gentle pace. 现存最大的古城门，明朝建造，距今600多年。在城墙上漫步，感受历史的厚度。城墙全长35公里，我们轻松走一段就好。

🚇 Pukou → Zhonghua Gate: Metro ~50 min 浦口 → 中华门：地铁约50分钟
~11:30

🍜 Lunch at Laomendong🍜 老门东午餐

One of Nanjing's oldest neighbourhoods — grey brick lanes, craft shops, and street food. We'll find an air-conditioned restaurant and try more local dishes. 南京最古老的街区之一——青砖小巷、手工艺店和小吃。找个有空调的餐厅，再尝几道南京菜。

🚶 10 min walk from Zhonghua Gate中华门步行约10分钟
12:00–15:00

😴 Midday rest😴 午休

Peak heat hours — back to the apartment. Richard can nap, read, or just enjoy the air conditioning. This is Nanjing survival strategy 101. 最热的时段——回公寓。Richard 可以午睡、看书、吹空调。这是南京生存基本法则。

~16:00

🏛️ Nanjing Museum🏛️ 南京博物院

One of China's top 3 museums. 430,000 artifacts spanning from ancient dinosaurs to Republic-era street scenes. Free entry, fully air-conditioned — perfect for a hot afternoon. 中国三大博物馆之一，馆藏43万件，从远古恐龙到民国街景。免费参观，全程空调——炎热下午的完美选择。

🚇 Pukou → Nanjing Museum: Metro ~45 min 浦口 → 南京博物院：地铁约45分钟
~18:30

🏮 Qinhuai River evening🏮 秦淮河夜景

"Misty water, moonlit sand" — the Tang poet Du Mu wrote about this river. We'll stroll along the lantern-lit banks and have dinner nearby. "烟笼寒水月笼沙"——唐代诗人杜牧写的就是这条河。沿着灯笼点亮的河岸散步，在附近吃晚餐。

Talking About Nanjing帮你聊南京帮你聊南京 Talking About Nanjing
Key English phrases and facts for each place we visit — so you can explain things with confidence. 每个景点的关键英文表达和知识点——让你讲解时更有底气。

🏯 City Wall城墙

Zhonghua Gate is the largest surviving city gate in the world. Built in the Ming Dynasty, over 600 years ago. The full wall is 35km — the longest ancient city wall on Earth.中华门是现存最大的古城门，明朝建造，距今600多年。全长35公里——世界最长古城墙。

Keywords: Ming Dynasty · 600 years · world's longest · 35km

🏛️ Nanjing Museum南京博物院

One of China's top 3 museums, alongside the Palace Museum and the National Museum. 430,000 artifacts. From dinosaurs to Republic-era street scenes. Free entry.中国三大博物馆之一（与故宫博物院、国家博物馆并列），馆藏43万件。从恐龙到民国街景。免费。

Keywords: top 3 museums · 430,000 artifacts · free entry

🏮 Qinhuai River秦淮河

Nanjing's "mother river." The Tang poet Du Mu wrote "烟笼寒水月笼沙" about its misty, moonlit beauty. It's been a cultural symbol for over 1,000 years.南京的"母亲河"。唐代诗人杜牧写下"烟笼寒水月笼沙"描绘它的朦胧月色。千年文化象征。

Keywords: mother river · Du Mu · Tang Dynasty · 1,000 years

☕ Keemun & Britain祁红与英国

Keemun black tea from Qimen, Anhui. Reached Britain in the 19th century, became a royal favourite. Much of British afternoon tea uses Keemun.祁门红茶产自安徽祁门，19世纪传入英国，成为皇室御用茶。英式下午茶很多用的就是祁红。

Keywords: Qimen · Anhui · 19th century · royal favourite

🦆 Nanjing & Ducks南京人吃鸭

Nanjing people eat over 100 million ducks a year. Saltwater duck has 2,000+ years of history. There's even a "Duck Museum" in the city.南京人一年吃掉一亿多只鸭子。盐水鸭有两千多年历史。南京还有个"鸭博物馆"。

Keywords: 100 million ducks · 2,000 years · duck museum
